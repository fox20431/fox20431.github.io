# XDG Standard

`.desktop` file path:

- `/usr/share/applications/`
- `~/.local/share/applications/`

autostart path:

- `/etc/xdg/autostart/`
- `~/.config/autostart`

`mime`(Multipurpose Internet Mail Extensions) setting:

- `/etc/mime.types`
- `~/.config/mimeapps.list`
